Dan weds Cords

Date: 22. 09. 2018 | Time: 11:00 | Location: London

#DanwedsCords

Their journey began thirteen years ago in the streets of Liverpool,
When a geeky dan had his braces and cords was still cool.
They have grown up with their love and also in other ways,
Cords engineering for big companies, dan taking dental xrays.
At the top of table mountain he asked her to be his wife,
There is nobody to whom he wanted to spend the rest of his life.
For all of you invited they wish you to attend and celebrate,
to witness cords walk down the aisle and marry her best mate.
Please help them both make this a day to remember,
We hope to see you all at the wedding come the end of September.
